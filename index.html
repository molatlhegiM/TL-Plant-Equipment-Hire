About Us

Excellence in Equipment Hire

TL Plant & Equipment Hire is a division of Pulse Management Consulting, specializing in reliable, premium, and versatile equipment hire across construction, agriculture, logistics, lifestyle transport, and event support services.

We combine corporate-level professionalism with a luxury-inspired service experience, offering clients a comprehensive fleet backed by trustworthy operations.

From heavy-duty machinery to luxury vehicles, we deliver quality, safety, and consistency to contractors, farmers, event planners, private clients, and corporate teams throughout Gauteng.
